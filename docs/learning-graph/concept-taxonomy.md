# Concept Taxonomy

This taxonomy organizes the 200 geometry concepts into 12 categories for better navigation and understanding.

## Categories

### 1. Foundation Concepts (FOUND)

**TaxonomyID**: FOUND

**Description**: The most fundamental building blocks of geometry including undefined terms (point, line, plane) and basic geometric objects. These concepts have minimal or no dependencies.

**Examples**: Point, Line, Plane, Ray, Line Segment, Angle

---

### 2. Logic and Reasoning (LOGIC)

**TaxonomyID**: LOGIC

**Description**: Concepts related to mathematical reasoning, proof techniques, and logical argumentation used throughout geometry.

**Examples**: Inductive Reasoning, Deductive Reasoning, Conditional Statement, Proof, Theorem

---

### 3. Angle Relationships (ANGLE)

**TaxonomyID**: ANGLE

**Description**: Classifications of angles and relationships between angles, including complementary, supplementary, and angles formed by transversals.

**Examples**: Acute Angle, Complementary Angles, Vertical Angles, Corresponding Angles

---

### 4. Constructions (CONST)

**TaxonomyID**: CONST

**Description**: Compass and straightedge construction techniques used to create geometric figures.

**Examples**: Compass Construction, Copying a Segment, Bisecting an Angle, Constructing Perpendiculars

---

### 5. Transformations (TRANS)

**TaxonomyID**: TRANS

**Description**: Geometric transformations including translations, rotations, reflections, dilations, and their compositions.

**Examples**: Translation, Rotation, Reflection, Rigid Motion, Congruent Figures

---

### 6. Lines and Planes (LINES)

**TaxonomyID**: LINES

**Description**: Relationships between lines, including parallel and perpendicular lines, and coordinate geometry of lines.

**Examples**: Parallel Lines, Perpendicular Lines, Transversal, Slope, Equations of Lines

---

### 7. Triangles (TRI)

**TaxonomyID**: TRI

**Description**: Triangle classifications, properties, congruence criteria, special segments, and centers.

**Examples**: Triangle, Isosceles Triangle, Triangle Congruence, SSS Congruence, Centroid

---

### 8. Polygons (POLY)

**TaxonomyID**: POLY

**Description**: Multi-sided figures including quadrilaterals, regular polygons, and their properties.

**Examples**: Polygon, Quadrilateral, Parallelogram, Rectangle, Pentagon, Tessellation

---

### 9. Similarity and Right Triangles (SIM)

**TaxonomyID**: SIM

**Description**: Similar figures, proportional reasoning, Pythagorean theorem, and trigonometric ratios.

**Examples**: Similar Figures, Scale Factor, Pythagorean Theorem, Sine Ratio, Indirect Measurement

---

### 10. Circles (CIRC)

**TaxonomyID**: CIRC

**Description**: Parts of circles, angles and arcs in circles, and equations of circles.

**Examples**: Circle, Radius, Chord, Tangent, Central Angle, Arc Length, Equation of Circle

---

### 11. Area and Volume (AREA)

**TaxonomyID**: AREA

**Description**: Formulas and concepts for calculating area of 2D figures, perimeter, circumference, surface area, and volume of 3D solids.

**Examples**: Area of Triangle, Circumference, Volume of Prism, Surface Area, Cavalieri's Principle

---

### 12. Applications (APP)

**TaxonomyID**: APP

**Description**: Real-world applications of geometry including modeling, design, optimization, and capstone projects.

**Examples**: Scale Drawing, Modeling with Geometry, Optimization Problem, Design Challenge

---

## Distribution Guidelines

- Foundation Concepts: ~10 concepts
- Logic and Reasoning: ~20 concepts
- Angle Relationships: ~15 concepts
- Constructions: ~8 concepts
- Transformations: ~12 concepts
- Lines and Planes: ~15 concepts
- Triangles: ~30 concepts
- Polygons: ~20 concepts
- Similarity and Right Triangles: ~20 concepts
- Circles: ~20 concepts
- Area and Volume: ~25 concepts
- Applications: ~5 concepts
