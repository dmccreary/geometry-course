## About This MicroSim

This interactive tool helps you find the complementary and supplementary angles for any angle you choose. Drag the slider to see how the angle pairs change.

## Key Concepts

| Pair Type | Definition | Sum |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| Complementary | Two angles that add to 90° | 90° |
| Supplementary | Two angles that add to 180° | 180° |

## How to Use

1. **Drag the slider** to choose any angle from 1° to 179°
2. **See the complement** (if it exists) - shown in green
3. **See the supplement** - shown in orange
4. **Read the summary** for the calculations

## Important Notes

- **Complementary angles** only exist when your angle is less than 90°
- Angles ≥ 90° have **no complement** (already ≥ 90°)
- **Every angle** has a supplementary angle

## Interactive Features

- **Real-time updates** as you move the slider
- **Visual representation** of both angle pairs
- **Automatic calculations** shown below

## Learning Objectives

- **Calculate** complementary and supplementary angles
- **Understand** the relationship between angle pairs
- **Recognize** when complements exist vs. don't exist
