## About This MicroSim

This interactive challenge guides you through constructing a parallel line using the corresponding angles method. Practice the steps and verify that the lines are truly parallel.

## Construction Steps

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1** | Draw a transversal through P intersecting ℓ at Q |
| **2** | Mark the angle formed at Q |
| **3** | Begin copying the angle at point P |
| **4** | Complete the copied angle (corresponding angle) |
| **5** | Draw line m through P - parallel to ℓ |

## Why It Works

When corresponding angles are congruent (∠1 ≅ ∠2), the lines are parallel. This is the **Corresponding Angles Postulate** in reverse!

## How to Use

1. **Step through** the construction using Next/Prev buttons
2. **Use Auto play** to watch the complete construction
3. **Notice the angles** - corresponding angles must be equal

## Learning Objectives

- **Construct** parallel lines using the corresponding angles method
- **Verify** parallelism through angle congruence
- **Apply** the corresponding angles theorem
