## The Equidistant Property

**Key Theorem**: Every point on the perpendicular bisector is equidistant from the endpoints of the segment.

This means for any point T on the perpendicular bisector: **TA = TB**

## How to Use

1. **Click through steps** to see the construction
2. **Click "Explore"** to enter exploration mode
3. **Drag point T** up and down along the bisector
4. **Watch the distances** - TA always equals TB!

## Learning Objectives

- **Construct** a perpendicular bisector
- **Verify** the equidistant property through exploration
- **Evaluate** why the construction creates both a bisector and a perpendicular
