## About This Diagram

This diagram shows two congruent triangles (△ABC ≅ △DEF) with all corresponding parts labeled. Click on table rows to highlight corresponding parts on both triangles.

## Corresponding Parts

### Sides
| Triangle ABC | Triangle DEF | Length |
|--------------|--------------|--------|
| AB | DE | 5 units |
| BC | EF | 7 units |
| AC | DF | 6 units |

### Angles
| Triangle ABC | Triangle DEF | Measure |
|--------------|--------------|---------|
| ∠A | ∠D | 60° |
| ∠B | ∠E | 80° |
| ∠C | ∠F | 40° |

## Key Concepts

- **Corresponding parts** of congruent triangles have equal measures
- **CPCTC**: Corresponding Parts of Congruent Triangles are Congruent
- Order matters in congruence statements: ABC ≅ DEF means A↔D, B↔E, C↔F
- Tick marks indicate equal sides; arc marks indicate equal angles
